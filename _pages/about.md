I am first year Ph.D. student at the [Vancouver School of Economics](https://economics.ubc.ca) at the University of British Columbia. My research interests lie in applied microeconomics problems. I try to answer this questions with both: theoretical and empirical tools. You can find my CV [here](https://pvalenzuelac.github.io/files/CV_Pablo_Valenzuela.pdf).

Prior joining UBC, I received a B.A. and a M.A. at the [Economics Department, PUC](https://economia.uc.cl). After that I worked as full-time research assistant in the same institution.
